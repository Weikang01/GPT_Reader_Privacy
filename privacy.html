Privacy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines the privacy practices for all interactions and functionalities provided by GPT Reader, a personal Chrome extension. It details the types of information that GPT Reader might deal with and how such information is treated.

Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via email at wliu29@lakeheadu.ca.

User Accounts

GPT Reader does not require the creation of a user account. Users can access all functionalities of the extension without providing any personal information.

Personal Information

GPT Reader does not collect, store, or process any personal information from its users. Your privacy is our priority, and as such, the extension is designed to function without needing to identify its users.

Cookies

GPT Reader utilizes cookies in a manner similar to Chat GPT Extension to enhance user experience. Cookies are small data files stored on your device to help improve the functionality of the extension. You may configure your browser to refuse cookies if you prefer, though this may affect your user experience with GPT Reader.

Data

The extension uses local storage technology to save user settings directly within your browser. This ensures that all data related to GPT Reader remains offline and under your control, enhancing privacy and security.

Analytics Data

We do not collect or process analytics data through Google Analytics or any other service. Your usage of GPT Reader remains private and is not monitored or analyzed.

Children's Privacy

We recognize the importance of protecting the privacy of young internet users. GPT Reader encourages parents and guardians to take an active role in their children's online activities to ensure a safe and secure browsing experience.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. Any changes will be reflected on this page, and we will update the "Last Updated" date below. We encourage users to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

Your Acceptance of These Terms

By using GPT Reader, you signify your acceptance of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to this policy, please do not use the extension. Your continued use of GPT Reader following the posting of changes to this policy will be deemed your acceptance of those changes.
